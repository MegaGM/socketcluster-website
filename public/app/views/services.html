Services

If you need professional assistance with the planning, design, development and/or evaluation of real-time systems built with SocketCluster, feel free to get in touch with us.

Each consultant listed on this page is an expert in real-time systems and has made significant contributions to the SocketCluster project. Some of the services we offer around real-time systems include:

  • Development of custom chat applications (including chatbots)
  • Development of simple multi-player games
  • Security and performance reviews of existing systems
  • Development of WebSocket-based REST/CRUD APIs
  • Integration with native mobile apps
  • Consulting related to architecture planning
  • Consulting and development related to deployment, infrastructure and scalability (specialising in Docker, Kubernetes, Rancher and AWS)
  • DevOps/ongoing infrastructure management
  • Migration from third-party pub/sub services to your own SocketCluster-based services
  • Technical support
  • Tutoring
